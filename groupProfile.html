Group Profile

Madhura group of companies was established in 1994. The first company launched was Madhura International. Madhura Controls & Automation Pvt. Ltd and Madhura Lubricants were launched at a later stage. Today, Madhura has a solid base of more than 50 OEM customers (Original Equipment Manufacturer). The customers are major manufacturers of Engine, Vehicle, Genset, and Panel across India and overseas. Madhura has earned the reputation of being a supplier of hi-tech products & services.

Technology

We use only the latest & state-of-the-art technology. Most of our products are microprocessor-based and produced in modern manufacturing facilities. Technology-driven products render optimal performance & longer life. The products are well-proven for harsh Indian conditions.
