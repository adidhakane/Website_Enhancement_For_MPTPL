Automatic Battery Monitoring System (BMS)

Our Automatic Battery Monitoring System (BMS) ensures real-time tracking of battery health, voltage, temperature, and charge cycles. Designed for industrial and commercial applications, it enhances battery life and efficiency by providing predictive maintenance alerts and remote monitoring capabilities.

Smart Power Distribution Unit (PDU)

The Smart Power Distribution Unit (PDU) is an advanced solution for managing power distribution in data centers and industrial setups. It features remote monitoring, load balancing, and real-time energy consumption analysis, helping businesses optimize power usage and reduce downtime.

Embedded Engine Control Unit (ECU)

Our Embedded Engine Control Unit (ECU) is a high-performance system designed for seamless integration into automotive and industrial engines. It offers precise fuel management, real-time diagnostics, and adaptive control mechanisms to enhance efficiency and reduce emissions.

Remote Generator Monitoring System

The Remote Generator Monitoring System allows real-time tracking of generator performance, fuel levels, and maintenance status. It enables remote diagnostics, alert notifications, and predictive maintenance, ensuring uninterrupted power supply for industrial and commercial applications.

Industrial IoT (IIoT) Data Logger

Our Industrial IoT Data Logger is a powerful device that collects, stores, and transmits critical operational data from machinery and industrial equipment. With cloud connectivity and analytics capabilities, it helps businesses improve efficiency, reduce downtime, and optimize resource utilization.
